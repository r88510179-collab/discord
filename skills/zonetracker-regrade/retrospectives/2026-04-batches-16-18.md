## 5. Recurring grader patterns

- **B's loss-profit-math bug (6 bets across B16/B17/B18).** B emitted `−(wager × decimal_payout − 1)` for losses instead of `−wager`. Affects bets `0f70d62c52b9`, `3ddc3ced6d7a`, `a9ceb4299a61`, `45a9e7406601`, `a9f57aa4814c`, `3ca460b2c1fc`. Every instance was on -110-odds losses (showed as −0.9091 instead of −1). Now caught by the validator in `methodology.md` §1 (Tightened rules from B16–B18).
- **A over-voids parlays where one leg voids and another fails.** Anti-pattern #4 violation: A returns `unknown` on a parlay because one leg is a void (cancellation/DNP) when another leg is verifiably failed. Failed-leg-kills-parlay still applies regardless of voids on other legs. Observed in B17 on the Cody 8-leg parlay (`a9ceb4299a61`) — A flagged Quickley DNP and walked back to unknown; B (and final) correctly identified Ingram's 7-pt line as the failed leg.
- **A's confidently-wrong evidence attributions.** B17 `e3550efcd198` is the canonical case: A wrote "Ohtani homered" supporting WIN, with an NBA-recap evidence_url and a Jokic-triple-double quote. The proper-noun mismatch between reason and quote is what the new evidence-quote validator (§3 of Tightened rules) catches.
- **Hours-vs-date precision in recap_tracker anchoring.** B18 `fb5911b62fe8` exposed this for the first time — same-day events that completed before the post still must anchor the bet, not the prior day's same-series event. Rule now in `methodology.md` §2 (Tightened rules).
- **Promo / referral / Sleeper-UD-Betr-Whatnot ingestion noise.** 25 of 65 bets graded `unknown` (38.5%) — but the substantive cause is concentrated: zrob4444's 4 rows were all UD/Sleeper/Betr promos, deeplaysbets's 1 row was an SGP-without-legs label, bookitwithtrent's 5 unknowns were Whatnot/YouTube promos and one ambiguous fade comment. That's 10 of 25 unknowns (40%) coming from accounts that should be filtered upstream. Surface to ingestion-bouncer roadmap as `UNGRADABLE_PROMO`.
- **Multi-name parlay convention applied correctly when odds non-null, mark unknown when null.** B18 `fb5911b62fe8` (odds populated, parlay confirmed) and B17 `f2658865470f` (truncated 8-name list, marked unknown) both followed the bobby__tracker convention from `references/capper-classes.md`. Convention is stable; the open issue is only what to do with hours-precision when anchoring (now resolved).
